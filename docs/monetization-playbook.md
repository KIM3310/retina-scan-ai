# Monetization Playbook - retina-scan-ai

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B non-clinical ML validation
- **Primary buyer:** Research groups, health-tech prototype teams, ML reviewers, and model governance teams.
- **Revenue wedge:** Medical-image research workflow with Grad-CAM and explicit non-diagnostic boundaries.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Validation template** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$10k validation study
2. **Explainability demo** - pricing hypothesis: $10k-$35k prototype review
3. **Model governance review artifact** - pricing hypothesis: $1k-$5k/month governance support

## Acquisition Channels

- ML governance content
- Grad-CAM demo
- Health-tech research outreach

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- pytest passes
- Model card/risk notes exist
- API inference path is documented

## Risk Boundaries

- Not a medical device
- No diagnosis claims
- Formal validation required for clinical use

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish model-card walkthrough
- **60 days:** Create validation checklist
- **90 days:** Offer explainability review

## Metrics That Matter

- Metric coverage
- Explainability artifact quality
- Risk register completeness

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
